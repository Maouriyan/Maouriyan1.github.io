Automated Automobile Exhaust Purification System

Device which minimises the effects of the harmful exhaust gases emitted from the automobiles and the industries.

Purification

Multiple effective filters are used to purify and reduce the harmful exhaust components such as Nox,CO2 and convert them into useful products.

Monitoring

The Monitoring leisure helps the users to maintain a record of the pollution caused by their vehicles/factories . Also the it keep an eye on the neighbouring vehicles and factories that have the device embedded with a network to predict the pollution affected areas to reroute users to environmentally safer zones when needed.

Pricing

The product is priced at Rs.5000 for all two-wheelers ,inclusive of two months of free chemical refills.
